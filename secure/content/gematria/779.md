## Greek/Latin:

---

### Hilasterion (Gr)
> The lid, cover. In the Septuagint it describes the cover of the Ark. In the English Bible, "Mercy Seat." Described by the Hebrew word KPRTh, Kapporeth. see [700](700), [180](180).
